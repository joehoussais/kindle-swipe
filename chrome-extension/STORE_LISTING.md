## Extension Name
Highlight - Kindle Sync

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Import all your Kindle highlights with one click. Syncs directly from Amazon to the Highlight app for easy browsing and discovery.

## Detailed Description
**Import your entire Kindle library in seconds.**

Highlight Sync connects to your Amazon Kindle notebook and imports all your highlights with a single click. No more manual copy-pasting or exporting files.

**How it works:**
1. Install the extension
2. Go to read.amazon.com/notebook (your Kindle highlights page)
3. Click the green "Sync to Highlight" button
4. Watch as all your books are synced automatically
5. Paste the data into the Highlight app

**Features:**
- One-click sync of ALL your Kindle books
- Works with 10+ Amazon regions (US, UK, DE, FR, ES, IT, JP, IN, AU, CA)
- Fast - syncs 100+ books in under 2 minutes
- Automatic clipboard copy for easy import
- Visual progress bar shows sync status
- Works with the free Highlight app at kindle-swipe.netlify.app

**What gets imported:**
- All your highlighted passages
- Your personal notes
- Book titles and authors
- Location markers

**Privacy focused:**
- No account required
- Your data never touches our servers
- Everything stays on your device
- Open source code

**Perfect for:**
- Readers who want to revisit their favorite passages
- Students organizing research highlights
- Writers collecting inspiration
- Anyone who highlights on Kindle

The companion Highlight app lets you swipe through your highlights like Tinder, rediscovering gems from books you've read.
